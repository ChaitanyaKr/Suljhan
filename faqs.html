How many openings are there between our legs? What is the purpose of each opening?

There are three openings between our legs. Urethra, from where the urine comes out; Vagina, from where the baby comes out during childbirth; and Anus, from where the feces come out.

What do we need iron for?

Iron is necessary for carrying Oxygen in the blood. Deficiency of iron causes Anaemia in which case you feel very weak even after taking rest.

What are the sources of Iron?

Peas, spinach, potato with skin, dal, nuts and dry fruits and fish

Why is it better to have Iron rich food with food rich in Vitamin C?

Having Iron rich food with food rich in Vitamin C increases the absorption of iron in the body.

What are periods? Is it normal?

Periods are a process during which blood comes out of the vagina for 3-7 days. It is normal.

Why does blood come out of vagina during periods?

In the absence of a baby, the uterus sheds its inner lining of blood and soft tissue, which we see as blood coming out of the vagina.

When do periods start and stop?

Periods generally start between the ages of 10 to 15 years and stop between the ages of 45 to 55 years.
