About


Adolescence is an age marked by doubts, fears and curiosities. It is to cater to these issues at one place, that we present Suljhan.

Various aspects relevant for an adolescent's awareness are covered in the app, under different sections. There is also a section to test the aolescent's learning.

Legal Frameworks help adolescent girls stay informed on various laws, rights and schemes.
It covers the following sub-sections:

  • Laws and Acts stated in the Constitution of India for safeguarding the rights and responsibilities of adolescent girls.

  • This section contains the fundamental rights chartered by the UN for adolescent girls all over the world.

  • Recent scemes like SABLA and ASHA initiated by the government of India





Health informs adolescent girls about various diseases.
It educates them about the following:

  • Symptoms: How to identify which disease someone has contracted.

  • Causes: What could be the possible reasons for someone being infected by the disease.

  • Treatment: Indicative idea about the correct medication.

  • Preventive: Measures to prevent the disease.


Hygiene and Sanitation educate adolescent girls about the importance of immunization., proper sanitation and menstrual health and hygiene.
It educates them about the following:

  • Menstrual awareness

  • Maintaining proper hygiene

  • Using a sanitary pad




How To provides everyday basic videos familiarizing adolescents with everyday banking services, Information and Technology and basic numeracy.
It educates them about the following:

  • Opening and saving a bank account

  • ATM card and its uses.

  • Sending out an email.

  • Registering for a voter card or an Adhar card.
